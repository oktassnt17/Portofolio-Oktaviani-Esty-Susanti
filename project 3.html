Analysis of the Effect of Population on the Number of Labor Force in Jombang Regency

Background of the Problem

Population growth in Kabupaten Jombang has the potential to affect the size of the labor force, which is an important indicator in supporting economic activity and regional development. As the population increases, it is expected that the size of the labor force will also increase, but the relationship between the two can be influenced by various factors such as education levels and employment policies. Therefore, it is important to analyze the effect of population on the size of the labor force in order to provide a solid basis for effective employment and economic development policy planning in Kabupaten Jombang.

Purpose of the Problem

This study aims to determine the effect of population on the number of labor forces in Jombang Regency in 2021.

Benefits of the Research

  • For Local Government : Provides the necessary information to design effective employment policies and economic development programs, and to manage population growth with appropriate strategies to improve community welfare
  • For other researchers : Serve as a reference for further research on the relationship between demographics and labor force, and provide data and findings that can be used for similar studies in other regions or in different contexts
  • For the Public : Help people understand how population growth affects employment opportunities, so that they can better plan their careers and education, and take advantage of opportunities

Project Results

Conclusion

Based on the analysis conducted, it was found that the population in Jombang Regency has a significant positive influence on the size of the labor force. The regression model equation shows that every one unit increase in population will increase the labor force by 0.3860. With an R squared value of 97.65%, this regression model explains most of the variation in the size of the labor force, indicating that the model is very good at predicting the relationship between population and labor force.

Recommendations

  • For Local Government : Develop skills training programs to tap into the potential of the rising labor force. In addition, investments in employment infrastructure such as career centers and job fairs are essential to facilitate the absorption of the new labor force into the job market. The government also needs to conduct regular monitoring and evaluation of the dynamics of the population and labor force to adjust policies appropriately and responsively
  • For other researchers : Conduct follow-up studies that explore other factors that affect the labor force, such as education levels, training, and employment policies. In addition, comparing the results of this study with studies in other regions can provide deeper insights into the relationship between demographics and the labor force and identify relevant patterns or differences
  • For the Public : Take advantage of available training and education programs to improve their skills and maximize employment opportunities. In addition, by understanding information about population and labor force growth, people can plan their careers and choose educational pathways that match labor market needs and opportunities
